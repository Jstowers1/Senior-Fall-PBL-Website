Problem Statement PSA Checker Extra Work

What's the problem anyway?!

I heard from the World Economic Forum that AI will replace 85 million jobs by 2025, is this true?!
Yes, that information is unfortunately correct. As AI quickly evolves, more workers will be put at risk.
Holy smokes... that sounds awful! Luckily, me and my IT friends are safe from being replaced, right?
I'm really sorry buddy, but unfortunately you and your IT friends are some of those who are at the greatest risk...
NOOOO!!!! YOU HAVE TO BE LYING! I CAN'T HAVE MY JOB BE REPLACED BY A COMPUTER!! I MADE AI!!
It will be okay! There's a group against AI who want to implement government changes to solve this issue!
They want to integrate AI, not get rid of it. AI works best as a tool instead of a replacement.
This system will have the federal government put code into an advance AI detection system to see an AI percentage.
The company can then earn a grant if the returned percentage is within a certain range, say 20-40%!
Woah, that sounds like a great system! I get to keep my job AND add a new powerful tool to my arsenal!
