A message from the founders:

We are a group of aspiring high school seniors with a desire to work in STEM career paths. With a majority of our group engrossed in software development, the issue of artificial intelligence replacing jobs causes concern. As students participating in STEM pathways, we understand the dedication and hard work that it takes to make it in these careers. As we approach college, not wanting the years of work to be stripped away by a complex computer program, we propose a solution that incorporates the use of AI into jobs rather than having a full human replacement.
